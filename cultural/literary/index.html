• Debate

    A debate is a contest where you learn to use a library, and to find the exact information needed in the shortest possible time. You learn to be thorough and accurate. You learn to analyse; to distinguish between the vital and the unimportant. You learn the need of proving your statements; of supporting every statement with valid evidence. You learn to present ideas in a clear and effective manner, and in a way which wins others to your way of thinking.

  • Poetry and Elocution

    Poetry is the most beautiful form for portrayal of ideas, cultures, emotions and dreams. Though a lot of styles compel one to adhere to a strict format, spoken word poetry leaves the pen unshackled and comes out to be the most liberating of all. Spoken word allows you to incorporate hip-hop, rap and abstract forms into your creation and present it strongly. Through this, the essence of the poetry also gets deep rooted in the audience.
